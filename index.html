What is RACISM?

Racism is any prejudice or discriminatory behavior against a person or group of people based on the belief that one's own race is superior. Racism can come in many forms, whether it be one's thoughts, words, or actions.


What is the difference between RACISM and PREJUDICE?

While prejudice is someone's unwarranted bias, racism comes from a place of power and oppression that is held over the people that are victims of the racism.
